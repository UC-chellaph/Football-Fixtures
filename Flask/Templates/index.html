Welcome to Football Fixtures

This program was designed by Prateek Chellani as part of a class project. It scrapes data from several credible Football websites, such as Transfermarkt and Sofascore to retrieves scores, results, fixtures and standings for the world's leading football league, the English Premier League

Supported Teams

At the moment, only the current top flight Premier League Teams are supported. A full list of these teams can be found by hovering over the teams tab.
